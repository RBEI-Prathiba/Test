BUSMASTER

BUSMASTER is an Open Source Software tool to Simulate, Analyze and Test data bus systems such as CAN, CAN-FD, LIN, FlexRay.

 

Download, run, study, modify and redistribute. Join the BUSMASTER community to benefit from the updates, bug-fixes and to contribute!
